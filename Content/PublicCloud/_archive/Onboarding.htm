Onboarding

has a built-in onboarding 'wizard' that gets you through the first steps, taking you from screen to screen. The wizard is popped up automatically the first time you log in to the system. Thereafter, you can run it whenever you like. Click on the Help icon at the top of the screen and select On-boarding from the menu.

Follow the instructions in the displayed panel. These are the steps covered:

  • Specifying your accounts in the Account Manager
  • Specifying how should use tags in the App Manager
  • Setting up your security policy rules
  • Creating custom tags that will let identify your applications
