Policy

The policy is a set of rules that define the connectivity you want to allow to and from your assets. 's global application visibility presents your cloud environment as applications, so you can see compliance with policy at the level of your business applications as well. Rules consist of source, destination and type of service, where source and destination are based on subnet, asset tags or other asset properties. Once your policy is defined in , you have a security baseline against which your cloud asset and application connectivity can be compared. will report any violations of the policy after considering all the relevant security entities and network connectivity.
