Sorting

Sorting Rows

When supported in a specific panel, a sort button will appear at the top of the panel. Click on the Sort button, and the supported sort criteria for that panel appears. Select the desired sort criteria and the data will be redisplayed accordingly.

Sorting by Column

Most tables in let you sort by any column. Click on a column header to toggle the sorting by that column in ascending or descending order.
