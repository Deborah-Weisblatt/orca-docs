Notification Settings

/

You can configure to send notifications. Supported notification methods are:

  • email - Enter a single email username.

  • Slack - Enter a Slack incoming Webhook URL, to send the notification to an individual or group.

    See Slack documentation for incoming webhooks for additional details.

  • Enable or disable notifications - Click the toggle button to enable or disable a notification.

    Enabled.

    Disabled.

  • Edit a recipient - Click and add or edit the recipient and click Submit.

From the menu, click Settings > Notification Settings
