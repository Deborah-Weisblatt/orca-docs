About Getting Started

This section gives you the steps you need to take before you can work with the system.

To get started with :

  1. Sign Up for an account at the Tufin Orca homepage.
  2. Deploy on your Kubernetes cluster.
  3. Go and Check It's Working.

After that, if you haven't already gone over the Concepts, you should do so now. Then continue with Working With Orca to learn how to use the application.
