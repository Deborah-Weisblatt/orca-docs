Risky Ports - Assets

The risky ports application view lists an application's assets that have one or more high-risk ports open.

What Are Risky Ports?

The selected application's name appears at the top of the screen. The main screen section is a list of the application's assets showing the following information:

  • Risky ports. A list of all risky ports identified in the asset
