Add Users

  1. Click .

    The Invite Teammate window appears:

  2. Enter the email of the user you want to add to the account.

  3. Click Send.

    An email invitation will be sent to the user containing instructions for joining the account. If the invitee joins the account, they will be added to the list of users.

Invitee Actions

You have received an email invitation to join the account.

  1. Click Join in the email message.

    The Join Account window appears.

  2. Enter your name and a password and click Join Account.

    You now have access to the account.

Delete Users

  • Click on the Ellipsis button for the user you want to remove.
  • Click Remove User from the pop-up menu.
