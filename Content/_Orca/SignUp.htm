Signing Up

If you want to join an existing account, ask one of your teammates to invite you from within

To sign up for a new Account

  1. Go to the Tufin homepage and click Sign up.

  2. Enter your corporate email address. A verification email will be sent to your inbox.

  3. Click on the verification link in the email. This will take you to the create account page.

  4. Enter a name for your new account, your name and password and click Create Account. If the details you entered are valid, the login screen will be displayed.

  5. On the login screen enter your account name and click Next.
  6. Enter your email and password and click Login.
