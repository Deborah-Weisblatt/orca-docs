Signing Up

To sign up for a Account

  1. Go to the Tufin Orca homepage and click sign up.

  2. Enter your email address. A verification email will be sent to your inbox.

  3. Click on the verification link in the email. This will take you to the create account page.

  4. Create an account by entering your name, project name and password. You can now log in to .

  5. Log in. You will see the instructions to install the agent on your cluster. You can click on Send to send the instructions to your inbox.

  6. Run the deployment command. If you cannot do this immediately, it can be done later. When run, will automatically connect to your cluster and an email notification will be sent to your inbox. For more information see Deploying ,

  7. Log out or close the browser window.

  8. After the deployment command has completed, log in again. You will see new connections discovered together with all the visibility provided by .
