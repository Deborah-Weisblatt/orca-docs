Configure a New Connection Alert

Get notifications via Slack when new connections are discovered.

  1. Enable / disable the alert on or off using the toggle switch.

  2. When the alert is enabled, enter the Slack URL of an individual or group to receive notifications - see Slack documentation. remembers the URL when disabled.
