CI/CD Pipeline

takes security policy automation right to your CI/CD pipeline with the following functions:

  1. Immutable Policy

    Replaces your cluster security policy with a brand new one as part of your CI build process. The new policy will allow only the connections that you expect from the latest build of your application and this policy gets deployed at the same time as the deployment of your application. See Immutable Policy.

  2. Container Image Scanning

    Runs a deep analysis scan on your container images automatically as part of your CI build process and calculates a . The result of the last scan performed is displayed in and can be displayed also in the form of a badge. See Image Scans in the CI/CD Pipeline.

Both functions require the addition of commands to your CI/CD script.
