CI/CD Pipeline

takes security policy automation right to your CI/CD pipeline with the following functions:

  1. Immutable Policy

    Your security policy is replaced with a new one as part of your CI build process. The new policy will contain only allowed connections made in a dedicated phase of your build process, and this policy gets deployed at the same time as the deployment of your application. See Immutable Policy.

  2. Container Image Scanning

    A deep analysis scan is performed on your container images as part of your CI build process and calculates a . The result of the last scan performed is displayed in and can be displayed also in the form of a badge. See Image Scans in the CI/CD Pipeline.
