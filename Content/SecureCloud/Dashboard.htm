Dashboard

The dashboard is a snapshot of your compliance with security policy and best practices in your cloud account settings and clusters. It is made up of s representing common risks, each showing a compliance score that indicates your robustness with respect to the risk.

Dashboard information will be available after you have specified public cloud accounts and information will be available after you have deployed on at least one (see Initial Setup). Until this has been done, the dashboard will display the relevant 'start' s instead of the info s.
