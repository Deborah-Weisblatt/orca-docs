Audit

All actions taken by users are logged.

The screen includes the following information:

  • Time, name and IP of the user who performed the action
  • Category - the context of in which the action was taken e.g. cloud settings, cluster policy
  • Resource - the sub-category or sub-context of in which the action was taken e.g. . For categories that have no sub-categories, the resource will be the same as the category.
  • Action - Add, Edit or Delete

  • View detail of audit item - hover over item and click

    To display the audit details window continuously, hover over the window title and click on the pin icon. Click again to cancel.

    To copy the json code to your clipboard, hover over the window and click on the copy icon .
