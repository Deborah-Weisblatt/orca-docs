Sign Up

A account typically represents a complete organizational network infrastructure, comprising its cloud vendor accounts and Kubernetes clusters. The first user signs up for and creates the new account and subsequent users must be invited to join the account by the original user.

To sign up for a new Account:

  1. Send us your details via the evaluation request form.

  2. Enter your email address. A verification email will be sent to your inbox.

  3. After making initial contact with our team, a verification email will be sent to your inbox.

  4. Click on the verification link in the email. This will take you to the create account page.

  5. Enter name for the account, your name and a password and click Create Account. Your account will be created and you can now log in.
