Select the Reports to be Sent

Mark one or more of the reports to be sent automatically to the selected subscribers. The reports are:

  • Security Posture
  • New Account Assessment
  • Public Cloud Violations
  • New Cluster Assessment

Security Posture

A weekly email report for and security posture that includes the most important categories of information from the . Once selected, this report will be sent every Tuesday at the time of day and to the subscribers you specify. (View example)

New Account Assessment Report

A one-time report sent 24 hours after a new account is added.

A daily report sent 7 days a week containing new in your assets at the time of day and to the subscribers you specify.

New Cluster Assessment

A report sent 24 hours after you connect a new cluster to that contains an analysis of the cluster security including , , and image vulnerabilities. After you have connected your first cluster, subsequent clusters can only be connected with the help of support.
