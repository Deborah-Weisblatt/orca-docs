This screen displays all policies in a selected namespace.

The selected cluster and namespace appear at the top of the screen. The main screen section is a list of Kubernetes network policies defined for the namespace, regardless of whether or not they are permissive.

  • Target Pods. The pods to which the policy applies, as specified under spec:podselector.
