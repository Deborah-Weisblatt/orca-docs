What is ?

is a security monitoring and management solution for public cloud and Kubernetes that gives you three key requirements needed for robust network security:

  • Visibility into your public cloud and Kubernetes environments that graphically displays elements and connectivity
  • Policy Management and control
  • Enforcement of your policy to secure the network

These capabilities help you identify security risks and address them fast. At the public cloud level, monitors and maps out your accounts and highlights deviations from security best practices. At the cluster level, gives you visibility into cluster workloads, connectivity and lets you enforce your security policy. Advanced automation features allow you to 'left-shift' security to your repository and CI/CD processes.

Policy

lets you define a global security policy for your that is checked against the effective connectivity set up in your cloud vendor accounts. automation then discovers violations and configuration issues in your environment. Similarly, in you can create a set of policy rules for your cluster. Building your cluster policy is made easy by using 'learning mode', which presents all new connections for your consideration and add them to the whitelist policy in a click. Read more in The Concept of Policy.
