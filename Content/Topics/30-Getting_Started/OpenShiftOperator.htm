Neil prefers to follow the rules below, so that the list of 'what I can do on this screen' is as compact, simple and uncluttered as can be:

  • Every action in the list above should be very brief, not extending beyond 1 line
  • Not to add any explanation in the list above, however short it is.
  • Always put explanations, however short they may be, in the section referred to. Including external links, which can be handled something like this: "To do this function, click here'. Even though it requires an additional keystroke compared to linking from the list directly to the target, we retain consistency of format.
  • Every action in the list above should link to a heading below with the exact same text, the only difference being that the first letter of each word of the heading will be capitalized.
