Kite

Kite is responsible for monitoring your Kubernetes cluster for changes, such as adding or deleting services, and for maintaining ongoing communication between your cluster and the service. It communicates information to and from the main Orca application on the cloud.

Kubernetes DNS plugin

The Kubernetes DNS plugin identifies and controls all communication between your services, as well as to external services. It identifies and controls communication based on DNS lookups (for example crm.mycompany.com). can also monitor and enforce service connectivity using Istio, which adds support for identification and control of communication through sidecar proxies. The DNS plugin is installed together with Kite.

See Installation of Kite and the DNS plugin on your Kubernetes cluster.
