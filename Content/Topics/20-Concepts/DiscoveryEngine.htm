Discovery Engine

The discovery engine is a continually running process that constantly monitors traffic to and from the services used by your application. Any traffic found that is not already listed as an allowed connection in your policy is added to the New Connections Window and the number of new connections is displayed at the top of the screen. Any connections discovered which are not listed as an allowed connection in your policy will be considered new connections.

You can view the New Connections Window by clicking New Connections at the top of the screen. See also Security Policy.

The discovery engine is always active, and runs independently of the Policy Mode. New connections can be converted to Allowed connections.
