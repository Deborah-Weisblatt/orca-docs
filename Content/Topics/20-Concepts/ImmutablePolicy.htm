Immutable Policy

provides you with a way to implement your security policy automatically and the Base mechanism employed for this is the creation of an immutable security policy. Immutable means fixed and unchangeable. That doesn't mean the fast-changing threat landscape isn't going to be met by equally rapid changes to your security rules, it just means that instead of modifying your security policy, you introduce a new improved one then dispose of the old one.
