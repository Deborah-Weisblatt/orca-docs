Policy

Types of Policy

There are two different kinds of policy in - the policy and the Kubernetes policy.

The Policy option is split into three distinct functions:

  • Building and maintaining the policy - add or remove allowed connections

  • Viewing Kubernetes network policies - see the pod-to-pod permissions currently defined in your Kubernetes policies

  • Viewing firewall tickets - see any currently open tickets from Tufin SecureChange

When clicking on Policy in the main menu, a sub-menu is displayed with options for each of these functions:

For building and maintaining the policy select Security Policy

For viewing the Kubernetes pod permissions select Kubernetes Network Policies

For viewing currently open firewall tickets select Firewall Tickets

Policy Mode

has 2 modes of operation or Policy Modes - Learn and Enforce. Learn mode lets you monitor your application without blocking traffic between services or containers so you can learn about the traffic connections between the services used in your Kubernetes cluster. The monitoring and visibility capabilities such as Service Graph, Pods, and Services are always available, and are independent of the mode setting.

  • Use Learn to build a Policy that is best suited to your application.
  • When you are satisfied that the policy meets your enforcement criteria, switch to Enforce and will start enforcing your policy.

employs a theme color specific to the current mode selected, which affects the top toolbar and some screen elements such as buttons. When in Learn mode, the theme is colored and when in Enforce mode, the theme is colored .
