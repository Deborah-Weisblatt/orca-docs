Working with Discovery

The discovery engine is a continually running process that constantly monitors traffic to and from the services used by your application. Any traffic found that is not already listed as an allowed connection in your policy is added to the New Connections Window from which you can easily add it to the policy.

You can view the New Connections Window by clicking New Connections at the top of the screen. See also Security Policy.

The discovery engine is always active, and runs independently of the Policy Mode .

What can I do?

  • View the New Connections list

  • Add a rule to the Policy - Select one or more rules in the New Connections List and click Add to Policy.

How do I get here?

From most screens, click on New Connections
