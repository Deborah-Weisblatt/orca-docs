Overly Permissive Rules Report

This report lists statistics and details of Kubernetes network policy rules that allow communication to and from your clusters without restriction of IP address or port. It is sorted by cluster and has the following structure:

  • Table of Contents - lists clusters and namespaces
  • Cluster1
    • Namespace1
    • Namespace2
    • ...
  • Cluster2
    • Namespace1
    • Namespace2
    • ...
  • ...

A summary of the types of rules and rule details are listed for each namespace.

  • View summary of rules
  • View details of rules
  • Download the report as PDF

View Summary of Rules

Click on the desired namespace in the table of contents or scroll down to it. The summary is displayed immediately after the namespace heading. The types of rule included are:

  • Rules allowing access from any port
  • Rules allowing access to any port
  • Rules allowing access from any IP
  • Rules allowing access to any IP

For each type of rule, the number of issues is displayed.

View details of Rules

Click on the desired namespace in the table of contents or scroll down to it. The details follow the summary and are grouped by type of rule. For an explanation of the information displayed, see Kubernetes Network Policies

Download the Report as PDF

Click on Download PDF at the top of the screen. The file will be downloaded to your default folder.

Click on the user icon at the top of the screen.

Click Reports from the menu displayed. The reports screen is displayed

Click on Overly Permissive Rules Report.

Exiting the Report

Return to the Main Menu by clicking the back button of your browser.
