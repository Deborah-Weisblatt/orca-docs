Discovery

The Discovery engine is always active, and runs independently of the mode setting (i.e. learn or enforce). The color of the discovery button changes, depending on the Learning Mode setting.

What can I do?

  • View the New Connections list - Click Discovery to display the New Connections list.

  • Add a rule to the Policy - Select one or more rules in the Connections List and click Add to Policy.
