Overview

The Firewall Tickets Screen lists change tickets that have been created by Orca in your network policy change management solution with the purpose of applying the security policy you have defined in to the devices managed by the network policy change management solution. It is available only if you have a policy change management solution such as integrated with . If you do not have this integration, it will not be possible to reach this screen.

After initiating the Apply on Firewalls process, one or more change tickets will be created in your network policy change management solution - one for each namespace in your policy - and they will be viewable on this screen.

Actions Available on this Screen

  • Viewing the firewall tickets
  • Linking to a ticket
  • General actions

Viewing the Firewall Tickets

View the firewall tickets that have been created by Orca in your network policy change management solution.

Field Description
Namespace The namespace in the cluster to which the rule applies
Endpoints The DNS name of source or destination outside of your cluster
Status One of four statuses. Initially Pending, then Running, then either Implemented or Failed.
Message Optional, depending on network change management systems
Ticket Unique ID provided by .
Last Update The date and time of the last status change your network policy change management solution

Linking to a Ticket

Click on a Ticket ID and you will be redirected to the appropriate ticket in your network policy change management solution.

How to Get Here

From the main menu select Policy then Firewall Tickets
