Firewall Tickets

The Firewall Tickets Screen lists change tickets that have been created by Orca in your network policy change management solution with the purpose of applying the security policy you have defined in to the devices managed by the network policy change management solution. It is available only if you have a policy change management solution such as integrated with . If you do not have this integration, it will not be possible to reach this screen.

After initiating the Apply on Firewalls process, one or more change tickets will be created in your network policy change management solution - one for each namespace in your policy - and they will be viewable on this screen.

Actions Available on this Screen

  • Viewing the firewall tickets that have been created by Orca in your network policy change management solution
  • Linking to the appropriate ticket in the network policy change management solution
  • Generic actions
