Policy

The Policy option is split into three distinct functions:

  • Building and maintaining the policy

    Add or remove allowed connections

  • Viewing Kubernetes network policies

    See the pod-to-pod permissions currently defined in your Kubernetes policies

  • Viewing firewall tickets

    See any currently open tickets from Tufin SecureChange

When clicking on Policy in the main menu, a sub-menu is displayed with options for each of these functions:

Select Allowed Connections for building and maintaining the policy

or Kubernetes Network Policies for viewing the Kubernetes pod permissions

or Firewall Tickets for viewing currently open tickets from

How do I get here?

  • Click Policy from the main menu.
