Policy

A policy is a whitelist or traffic rules describing the communication that you allow between the services in your application. Policies are enforced by between services. Policy enforcement only occurs if learning mode is disabled. See Learning Mode Settings for configuration details.

What can I do?

  • Delete an allowed connection - Hover over a connection and click .

  • Filter - Filter by: namespace

How do I get here?

From the menu in the left panel, click Policy.
